What we Offer

Psycho Threapy

Psychothreapy also called as talk threapy refers to a variety of treatments that aim to help a person identify and change troubling emotions, thoughts, and behaviors.

Most Psychothreapy takes place place when a licensed mental health professional and a patient meet one-on-one or with other patients in a group setting.

Behavioural therapy

Behavioral Threapy is a form of therapy to identify and help change self-destructive or unhealthy behaviors.

It's based on the idea that behaviors are learnt and behaviors can be changed.

Congnitive Behavioural Therapy

Cognitive behavioural threapy is a talking therapy that can help manage your problems by changing the way you think and behave.

It's commonly used to treat anxiety and depression and it can also be useful for other mental health problems and physical health problems
